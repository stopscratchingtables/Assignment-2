Build Your Own Starting 5!

'Build Your Own Starting 5!' is a mini-game on our site where you can create your own starting lineup team! Be as creative as you can and creat a starting five that can dominate the whole NBA!

How to Play the Game

  1. Enter the Name of a Player
  2. Press Submit
  3. Repeat Steps 1 and 2 together 4 more times
  4. Calculate the total statistics of the team
  5. Look at the gradings to see how well-built your lineup is!


If you occur any problems with a certain player, it is either a spelling problem or the API does not include the current player as of now
